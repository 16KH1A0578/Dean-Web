— Frequently asked

Questions, answered

Are you fully insured?

Yes. Roluxa carries comprehensive public liability and key-holder insurance. Documentation is shared on request.

How often will you visit my property?

As often as you need. Many empty-home clients choose weekly or fortnightly visits to satisfy insurance requirements; holiday-home owners often prefer monthly inspections plus arrival prep.

I live overseas — can you still help?

Absolutely. A large part of our work is for owners based abroad. We send detailed photo and written reports after every visit, and we're available across timezones via WhatsApp and email.

Do you provide one-off services or only contracts?

Both. We offer one-off deep cleans, garden tidy-ups and key-holding visits, as well as ongoing management contracts.

How do you handle keys and security?

Keys are held in coded storage off-site, never labelled with your address. All staff are vetted, uniformed, and follow strict security protocols.
